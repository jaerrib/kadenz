What can Kadenz do for you?

Kadenz was born out of personal experience with high school marching band boosters and a desire to make it easy to manage your organization's fundraising events. We're starting out small. Right now, you can post events like car washes or can drives, but many exciting features, like online sales campaigns, are planned for the future.
